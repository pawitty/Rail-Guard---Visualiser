RailGuard Radar Monitoring Dashboard

RailGuard uses millimeter-wave radar to detect structural movement in railway tracks. This dashboard compares two approaches: Baseline FFT magnitude detection and Phase-based displacement estimation. The aim is to demonstrate how phase tracking can enable sub-millimeter movement detection.
