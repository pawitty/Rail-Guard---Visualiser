RailGuard Radar Monitoring Dashboard

RailGuard explores the use of millimeter-wave radar to detect structural movement in railway tracks. The system uses the TI IWR6843 radar sensor to measure displacement between two reflective targets. The dashboard compares two approaches: • Baseline FFT magnitude detection (OOB firmware results) • Proposed phase-based displacement estimation The goal is to demonstrate how phase tracking enables detection of sub-millimeter structural displacement.
